This program convert the Tycho Input Catalog, 1/197A Revised version (Egret+ 1992) 
for use with "Cartes du Ciel" , "Sky Charts" program.
In the first field enter the complete directory path where you put the files 
tic1, tic2, tic3, tic4 of the original catalog ( keep this files in Unix fomat, 
do NOT convert them in cr+lf format). 
In the second field enter the path where to install the converted catalog, 
I suggest you keep the default : <Sky Chart path>\cat\tic
You must have 50 MB free disk space on the destination directory.
Press "Conversion" key and be patient... there are 732 files and 3'154'204 stars. 
This may take from ten minutes to more than one hour, depending on your machine. 
When it finish press "Exit" key.
