This program convert the Tycho Catalogue I/239 (ESA 1997)
for use with "Cartes du Ciel" / "Sky Charts" program.
In the first field enter the complete directory path where you put the file 
tyc_main.dat of the original catalog ( keep this file in Unix fomat, 
do NOT convert it in cr+lf format). 
In the second field enter the path where to install the converted catalog, 
I suggest you keep the default: <Sky Chart path>\cat\tycho
You must have 25 MB free disk space on the destination directory.
Press "Conversion" key and be patient... there are 732 files and 1'058'332 stars. 
This may take from ten minutes to more than one hour, depending on your machine. 
When it finish press *Exit" key.
